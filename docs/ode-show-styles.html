Three treatments of the same ODE outputs. Math is the real rendered LaTeX.

Style A — quiet math

Closest to today, just cleaned up. Everything stays typographic: the equation leads, and the classification / boundary / method are quiet serif lines beneath it — no boxes, no color. Calm and paper-like.

Style B — badges & steps

The semantic metadata becomes pill badges (kind, order, linearity, homogeneity, boundary, method) so the type of problem reads at a glance, and solve(details) turns into a numbered step list with a short label on each line. Most scannable.
